🎬 Intervision 01 — Los Angeles

Los Angeles, California, known as the city of dreams, will serve as the host city for the first Intervision Song Contest. A melting pot of cultures, languages, and creative expression, LA perfectly captures the spirit of global unity through music. From the Hollywood Hills to the vibrant downtown, Los Angeles will provide a dazzling stage for all 42 competing nations to shine.
