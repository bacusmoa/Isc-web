About the Aerivision Song Contest

The Aerivision Song Contest is a thrilling musical competition hosted within the Roblox community. Open to just 6–8 elite participants, this event challenges singers to perform a song of their choosing in front of a voting audience.

Established to spotlight talent, creativity, and charisma, Aerivision delivers the ultimate stage to shine — and win over 2000 Robux in prizes!

Each edition aims to bring people together through music, celebration, and friendly competition.
