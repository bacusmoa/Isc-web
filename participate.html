Interested in joining the contest? Follow the rules and submit your entry using the form below.

ENTRY RULES

  • Song must be under 4 minutes.
  • No slurs or too much profanity.
  • Must not include hate speech.
  • You must have little to no background noise while on stage.
  • Music must be loud and clear. External speakers allowed.
  • You cannot submit your own song in this competition. This option will become available in future contests.
  • Music must be instrumental.
  • Singing a capella is allowed.

SINGER RULES

  • You can only be participating by yourself.
  • No manipulating votes (bribing, convincing, blackmail, etc.).
  • Be kind and respectful to other singers.
  • Must always respond to updates about the contest.
  • All requests made by Director General (Aeri), must be followed.
  • Be Non-political.
  • Be on time to the contest.
  • Failing to comply with our Discord Guidelines, singer rules, or Discord ToS, may disqualify you.
